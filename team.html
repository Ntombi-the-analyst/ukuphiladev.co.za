Our Team

Meet the dedicated individuals who make Ukuphila KwakaNdengezi Development possible.

Pastor Lamula

Chairperson & Co-Founder

Pastor Lamula has dedicated his life to serving the Kwa-Ndengezi community, providing spiritual guidance and practical support to youth and families in need. With years of experience in community development, he leads our organization with vision and compassion.

Lady Pastor Lamula

Co-Founder & Women's Program Director

Mrs Lamula leads our women's empowerment initiatives, providing emotional support, business training, and spiritual encouragement to women in the community. Her passion for empowering women drives our successful programs.
