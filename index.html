About Us

A Christ-centered NPO based in Ndengezi that empowers youth and women through education, spiritual growth, and sustainable income projects.

Ukuphila KwakaNdengezi Development stands with the people of Kwa-Ndengezi ‚Äî we combine education, spiritual guidance and sustainable income projects to create lasting change. We welcome you to partner with us in transforming lives.

Our Mission

To empower youth and women through practical skills training, mentorship, emotional support and community-led income projects that restore dignity and create opportunity.

Our Vision

A thriving Kwa-Ndengezi where every young person and woman has access to education, spiritual formation and sustainable livelihoods to reach their full potential.

Our Values

  • Faith in action
  • Education as empowerment
  • Community-centered solutions
  • Sustainable development
  • Dignity & respect for all
